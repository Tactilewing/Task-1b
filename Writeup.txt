This site map is designed to feel like walking through a well-organised market, where everything is visible, inviting, and just a few steps away.
At its heart, the structure splits naturally into two worlds: customers and producers, with shared spaces like the Home, About, and Shop acting as the lively town square where everyone crosses paths.
The Home page acts as a welcoming storefront window, showing off featured goods and friendly faces. It gently guides users toward browsing products or learning about the people behind them. Nothing overwhelming, just enough to spark curiosity.
The Shop section is the engine room for customers. It’s designed for clarity and ease, with filtering options helping users quickly find what they want without rummaging endlessly. Real-time availability keeps things honest, reflecting the nature of small-scale production where stock can be limited and precious.
The Producers section adds personality and trust. Rather than faceless transactions, users can explore individual stories, sustainability practices, and production methods. This strengthens the community feel and aligns with the market’s not-for-profit ethos.
The My Account area gives users control over their experience, from tracking orders to revisiting past purchases. Alongside this, the Rewards section adds a gentle incentive layer, encouraging repeat engagement without feeling pushy.
For inclusivity, the Help & Support section ensures that users of all digital confidence levels can navigate the platform comfortably. Accessibility isn’t treated as an afterthought but as a built-in feature of the experience.
Finally, the Producer Portal is a separate, secure environment tailored to members. It allows them to manage listings, monitor inventory, and process orders efficiently. This keeps the operational side cleanly separated from the customer-facing experience, while still supporting the overall ecosystem.
Altogether, the structure balances simplicity with depth. It keeps the journey intuitive for customers while giving producers the tools they need to thrive.
